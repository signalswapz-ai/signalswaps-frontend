Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on our websites and apps

  1. What cookies are
    Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Similar technologies help us run the service securely, remember settings, and understand how the product is used.
  2. What we use them for
    We use strictly necessary cookies so the service works. Where allowed, we may use performance, functional, and advertising or measurement cookies to improve experience and measure campaigns.
  3. How long they last
    Session cookies are removed when you close the browser. Persistent cookies stay for a set period.
  4. Your choices
    You can control non-essential cookies through preferences and your browser/device settings.
  5. Changes to this policy
    We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time.
